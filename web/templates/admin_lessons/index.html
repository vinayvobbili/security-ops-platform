📖 How this dashboard works

Every quiz an analyst submits is logged with its score as a percentage, the questions they were shown, and a timestamp. This page rolls those attempts up per lesson.

  • Best score — the analyst's highest percentage across all their attempts on that lesson.
  • Passed — scored {{ pass_pct }}% or higher at least once.
  • 🏆 Distinction — best score reached {{ dist_pct }}% or higher.
  • Pass rate — share of analysts who attempted the lesson and passed it.
  • Avg best — average of every attempting analyst's best score.
  • ⚠️ Integrity — a passing attempt finished faster than it realistically takes to read and answer that test's mix of questions (open written answers count for much more time than multiple-choice). It flags possible answer-sharing or automation, not a verdict — Too fast is well under a quarter of the realistic floor, Review is under half. Failed attempts and attempts logged before timing was captured are never flagged.

Each lesson draws a fresh random set of questions from a large bank every attempt, so two analysts rarely see the same test — re-attempts are encouraged and only the best result counts. Click any analyst to see their full attempt history.

Times shown in US Eastern.
