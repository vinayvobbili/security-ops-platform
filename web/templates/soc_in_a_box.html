How does this work?

Every worked case is indexed with its strong indicators. The radar links two cases into a campaign when they share the same actor, named campaign, file hash, domain, IP, or CVE — and grows the cluster transitively (A↔B, B↔C ⇒ one campaign). A named-actor match counts even at two cases; otherwise a cluster needs at least two. First flagged / Last come from the alert ledger, so a campaign that keeps growing shows when it was first seen and most recently re-flagged. Read-only — no tickets are created and no action is taken.
