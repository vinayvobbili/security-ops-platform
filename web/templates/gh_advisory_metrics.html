How are these metrics computed?

🗃️

One backend, one feed

Everything here is aggregated from the advisory database that powers the queue. The exact same payload is served at /api/cs-advisories/metrics.json — point Power BI, Grafana, or any BI tool at it for an always-fresh feed instead of a screenshot.

📋

Scope

All counts are over the live queue (archived and baseline rows excluded), except the Archived KPI. Severity, source, status and ecosystem are simple group-bys.

⏱️

Operating tempo

Time to pick up = median hours from first-seen to a triager taking ownership. Time to notify = median hours from first-seen to escalation. Escalated rate = share of the queue escalated. Medians, so a single slow item doesn't skew them.

🛡️

Exposure-confirmed

Of advisories with a Veracode SCA result, the share that actually carry an affected component in our environment. A miss means no open SCA finding references the package — strong, but not absolute proof of absence from every SBOM.

✅

Team validation coverage

Per validating team: how many advisories they've marked clear / not-clear / still pending. Fully cleared = every enabled team has signed off clear.
