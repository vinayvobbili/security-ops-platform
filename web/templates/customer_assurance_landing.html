How the process works

1

Intake

Account team submits a customer questionnaire. Paste the questions or upload the file.

2

Auto-split

Questions are extracted into individual items, grouped by section.

3

Draft with AI

the LLM retrieves relevant policies, SOC 2 evidence, and standard responses, then drafts each answer with citations.

4

Review & Edit

Analysts review each draft, edit freely, approve, or flag for Legal / SME review.

5

Deliver

Export the final response as a Word document and send back via the account team.
